Music

Mrs. Anne Simpson teaches music to all ages at Oak Hill. She aims to develop the students physically and academically and to fulfill the biblical mandate to sing to the Lord.

Reading

Nearly all four year olds in our preschool program learn to read using our proven phonics method while Kindergarten students typically read on a third grade level by the end of the year.
