Schedule a Visit

We offer in-person tours at our Herndon Campus Tuesday-Thursday. Email us at office@oakhillk12.com to schedule a tour on a different day.

If you are out of town or prefer not to come in, Zoom or Facebook Messenger tours are available at all the times that in-person tours are available. Simply:

  1. Select your time.
  2. If you would like a tour by Zoom, indicate this in the message section and also tell us what email to send the Zoom link to.
  3. If you prefer Facebook Messenger, indicate this in the message section and tell us what email to send the Facebook Messenger room link to.
  4. Your personal tour guide will send a link to the Zoom call or the Facebook Messenger room a few minutes before your tour.
