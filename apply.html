New students please email the following documents to office@oakhillk12.com:

  1. Current report card if entering first grade or higher.
  2. Also include the previous year's report card if entering second grade or higher.
  3. Students entering high school should include records of all credits earned.
  4. Standardized test scores if available.
  5. A copy of your most recent Virginia health form may be included with this application or sent later.

By signing the online application, you indicate the following:

  1. You have received the 2020-2021 tuition schedule and agree to the terms and conditions.
  2. You authorize Oak Hill Christian School to obtain medical care deemed necessary for your child in the event that you cannot be located immediately.
  3. Pictures and videos of your child may be included in materials such as school yearbooks, websites, and promotional materials.
