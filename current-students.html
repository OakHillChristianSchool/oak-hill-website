Jupiter Grades

Parents or students can view grades or submit assignments through Jupiter Grades. Log in here.

Making Payments

Checks can be mailed to Oak Hill Christian School at 13525 Dulles Tech Dr, Suite 101, Herndon, VA 20171 or left in the dropbox on the front desk.

The registration fee can be paid via credit card if re-applying via the online application.

Payments can also be sent using Zelle, however, keep in mind that most banks have a limit of ~$2000/day. If your bank supports Zelle, you can send payments to headmaster@oakhillk12.com.

Online Classes

Before class, your child's teacher will send out a link to join an Adobe Connect classroom. Use that link to join online classes.
