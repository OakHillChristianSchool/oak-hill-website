F.A.S.T. — Financial Aid for School Tuition

Families who have a strong interest are encouraged to apply for admission regardless of means. Partial scholarships are funded by the school and are available based on need, available funding, and the family’s commitment to the mission of the school. Awards may range from $1,000 to a full tuition scholarship. Application should be made online at the link below with Independent School Management’s FAST program. Once a confidential application has been completed FAST will make a recommendation to the school.

Deadlines

Early Application: January 31

Application Deadline: April 30

Late Application: After April 30

Parents who wish to apply after June 1 should contact the office in advance and prior to submitting materials to indicate their interest and to determine if space is available.

New Student Admissions Procedure

  1. Visit Oak Hill if you are able. Bring your child with you if you wish.
  2. Complete the application for admission and pay the registration fee.
  3. For students entering grades 1-12, send us standardized test scores and the past two years’ report cards.
  4. We will contact you to schedule any needed testing or interview of the student.
  5. Applying for financial aid? Follow the same process but submit your application without the registration fee. Once you receive a financial aid decision you can pay the registration fee.
