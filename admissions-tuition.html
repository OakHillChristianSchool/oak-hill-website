Tuition is not the only thing because it is our desire to help build godly families. If you may have a strong interest in Oak Hill but tuition is above your budget, we encourage you to pursue enrollment anyway. We are happy to discuss scholarship options.

Even if tuition is in your budget, we know that it is a sacrifice on your part. Since we constantly hear that something can be had for nothing, it is easy to overlook the hidden costs of "free" education or the price of not finding academically rigorous and authentically Christian education.

OH scholarships are funded solely by the school and are available based on need, available funding, and the family's commitment to the mission of the school.
