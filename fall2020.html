In Person Teaching

Oak Hill is committed to being open this fall 9 a.m. – 3 p.m., 5 days a week for your child. Students need to work alongside their peers. Especially in Kindergarten and Grades 1-6, they need instruction from a teacher who meets them in person. Children pay the price when schools close more often than is necessary.

Socialization & Outdoor Time

We’re modifying schedules to ensure two outdoor playtimes every day. Fresh air, running with friends, climbing on the playground, all these are healthy and important especially after months of lockdowns.

Safe & Clean

Oak Hill is a member of the Association of Classical and Christian Schools as well as the Virginia Council for Private Education. Our reopening plan meets state guidelines. Desks are spaced generously in our large classrooms. We disinfect surfaces and clean our building throughout the day. As they always have at OH, our students wash hands and eat lunch brought from home in their own classrooms. Groups are not mixed.
